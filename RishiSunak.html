Everything to know about Rishi Sunak

  • Written by Sanju
  • Tagged as Blog

It's our challenges and obstacles that give us layers of depth and make us interesting. Are they fun when they happen? No. But they are what make us unique. And that's what I know for sure... I think.

The story of the 39-year-old Rishi Sunak is indeed one to be recounted several times. Popularly known as Maharaja of the Dales, he is the second youngest MP to take on the role of Chancellor of the Exchequer. The declaration was a result of the recent Boris Johnson’s cabinet reshuffle.

It's our challenges and obstacles that give us layers of depth and make us interesting. Are they fun when they happen? No. But they are what make us unique. And that's what I know for sure... I think.

Although the decision has surprised a few, the merit shown by the young Rishi Sunak is indeed that of a rising star. He is already well appreciated for his sharp brain, charming demeanor, and acute financial sense. Coming from the Yorkshire seat, his hometown, Rishi was Winchester educated, then went on to Oxford, and then Stanford for further studies.

He is married to the vivacious Akshata Murthy, daughter of Infosys co-founder, Narayana Murthy. A businesswoman in her own right, she owns ‘Akshata Designs’ a much appreciated and sought after designer label. They have two daughters.

Rishi Sunak himself is a respected businessman who set up a private investment partnership, Theleme Partners. This is after his stints with various investment firms, which include Goldman Sachs. It was when he was volunteering for the Conservatives and working with hedge funds that he took a step towards politics. In May 2015, he was elected as MP for Richmond in Yorkshire. As a Brexiteer, his influence and confidence was well-noted by Boris Johnson and helped him establish his stardom in politics.

The journey that the man undertook was not an easy one. One has to acknowledge the grit and determination showed by Rishi Sunak in not only securing a foothold in British politics but a strong position in the cabinet as well. His story certainly seeks to inspire many aspiring youngsters. After all, being dubbed as the ‘Prime Minister-in-waiting’ is not a simple task.
