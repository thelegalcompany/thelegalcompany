What is a Partnership Firm ?

A business structure in which two or more individuals operate and manage a business in accordance with the terms and objectives set out in a Partnership Deed that may or may not be registered, is called a Partnership Firm. In such cases, the members are individual partners who share the liabilities as well as the profits of the firm in a predetermined ratio.

Documents of Required



PAN Card

A self-attested copy of PAN Card of all partners

Partners Address Proof

Self- attested copy of Aadhar Card and Voter ID/ Passport/ Driving License of all partners

Business Address Proof

Utility Bill (Electricity Bill) of the place of business

Rent Agreement

Rent Agreement and NOC from the owner of the place of business, if rented
